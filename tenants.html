To Keep the Record of the Tenants

Commudum Lease is a platform that helps the property managers / owners to simplify their property management.

Project Overview

Commudum Lease is an application that helps the property owners / managers to keep and maintain the record of their tenant’s data to reduce their confusion as they might have many properties to look after. It will also save their time as they have a busy schedule.

Software Used

Adobe XD, photoshop, Illustrator, Figma

My Role

UX/UI Designer

Team Members

Jaspreet Kaur (Des), Tainara De A.D. (UX Des), Hung Ha (UX Des)

Problem

Due to the large number of the tenants and their data, it is difficult for the property managers to keep track of their tenant’s record. It is very difficult to keep record of all these having a busy schedule and they might have a large number of properties which can lead to confusion and misplace of some data.

Solution

Commudum Lease will help the property owners / managers to keep the record of all the tenant data with all their personal information, payment, contract expiry and create the report of their data. They can also download their report as a pdf.

User Flow and Wireframes

I and my team created the userflow of the application to start with the project. This was the team project for the UX class. The team discussed about the features for the application and created the wireframes. I helped the team to create the mobile version of the application after they completed the desktop version for the application.

UI Kit and Logo

After creating the wireframes with the help of my team, I created theUI kit and the logo for the webite. The logo is basically the “C” letter of Commudum + “L” letter of Lease and the “Home” icon and I combined it in such a way that its outer line completes the circle. I used the Drak Cyan, sunray and Spicy Mix colors as gradient for the logo.

I used Dark Cyan as the primary color with Parrot pink and spicy Mix as secondary colrs for the website. I used Josefin Sans typeface for the website because of its good legibility. I created the icons round edges and used the text to make the users easily understand the icons.

Mockups

I created the mockups for the same wireframes for the graphics class after the UI kit and the logo were designed.

Home Page

This is the home page when the users login or sign up for the website. They can add new tenant, go to payment page to check the pending and already paid payments of the tenants, go and check their contracts and their expiry dates. Users can also go on the report page and create and download the report too.

Add Tenant

When the user click on the “Add Tenant: card, this page will show where they can add all the information of the tenants and then save it.

All Tenants

Users will see the list of all tenants on tenants page, they can also check the list according to the floor number to shorten the list. They can also delete, export and edit the tenant’s information by clicking their name or multiple profiles by clicking the select button and choose their profiles.

Payment

When the user click on the “Payment” card on the home page, this page will show where they can check the list of the tenants and their payment information based on who are late to pay the rent and who already paid on time.

Contract

When the user click on the “Contract” card on the home page, this page will show where they can check the list of the tenants and their contract information based on whose contract is expired and whose is active with pdf attached.

Make Report

When the user click on the “Report”, this page will show where they can make the report for the tenants that includes payment, contract, room,etc and export it as a pdf.
