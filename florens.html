A Pocket expert for B.C.

Florens create a connection between British Columbia residents that are driven by our nature to a single platform that compiles all B.C flora profiles, where they can share their findings and create their own library based on their surroundings.

Project Overview

Florens facilitate and encourage nature lovers to share their findings on the application with the credit given to them with the plant profile, collect the plants they are more interested in by adding them to their library and also ease the usability from the expert to the regular user by using the search feature with advanced filters to find the plants easily.

Problem

British Columbia is home to the highest biodiversity in North America and has more than tens of thousands of plants in a rich ecosystem. It is very difficult to find out about them easily on the internet as it is very elaborated and a long list which took so much time to figure out the right information.

Solution

Our solution is a web app that compiles all information about our flora, where each tree or flower has a profile with photos and relevant information. People can use a search feature to type the data they are looking for, or use the filters to narrow down information and even add the plants to their library.

Home Page

Users can search using filters to narrow down the home page. Users will be able to see all the main features along with the cards that will tell the user about the plant of the day which is a random plant. At the bottom of the home page, a list of the plants according to the season will be displayed. Users can click on the plant image if they want to check its details and even save the plant to their library to check later on.

Advanced Search

Users are able to search for any plant using the advanced search feature which will allow them to narrow down the search. If the users do not know the name of the plant then they can search using filters such as season, color, texture, location, etc.
