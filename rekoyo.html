REKOYO: Spread the Art of Helping

REKOYO is the solution to finding someone who can share their experience and skills with others who need guidance.

Project Overview

REKOYO is an android-based mobile application that connects young people with retired and skilled people. Users who seek guidance can get it by posting statuses, and chatting with other users. They can categorize their connections and refer them to others who need help.

Problem

Research says that retirement can result in anxiety and stress and can be boring. Retired do not have anyone to talk to as everyone is busy in their lives. They are skilled and have lots of experience which they would like to share with others, especially youngsters to give them the right direction as they have a lack of direction these days, but there is no such platform where both generations can easily connect.

Solution

Retired people can utilize their free time to help youngsters on topics they need help with, this way they will not feel alone and share their experiences with them to give them the right direction.

REKOYO provides the solution to both youngsters and retired/experienced to connect with each other to take guidance and share their experiences. Users can connect and chat with each other for easy communication and help. They can refer the users to others to help them.

User Flow and Wireframes

The team decided on all the features keeping in mind all the problems faced by the users so that we can solve those with the help of our application. I created the user flow with the team member’s help keeping in mind easy interaction for the user. After the user flow, We created the wireframes and kept them simple and easy for the users to interact with, that the text is easily readable.

UI Kit and Logo

We used medium electric blue as primary with Spanish orange as secondary color because these colors represent importance and confidence (blue) and give energy and cheer you up (orange). Another reason to use blue as the primary color is that it has good contrast with white color and the orange color is bright which we used for text that is clickable to catch the users’ attention.

We used the geometric sans serif Montserrat typeface because of its good X-height and easy readability.

I created the icons with round edges and used the buttons with icons keeping in mind that these can be easily recognizable by the users. The logo is created using the same color palette with a lighter shade of blue and a gradient, white background, and text with Montserrat font type and kept lowercase to make the logo simple, bright, and eye-cathcing.

The logo of the application is created keeping in mind its main feature “Connection”, I created the connect icon first using a circle and three users representing small filled circles which as a whole represents the users connecting with each other as if they are joining their hands. So, the logo is a modification from that icon by removing three small circles and keeping it to a simple circle but broken into two halves so that it looks similar to both ‘O’ and the ring of ‘R’.

Mockups

Using the UI Kit, I and my fellow designers created High-fidelity Mockups.

Posting status

Users can post status on the home page by clicking on the input box of status and typing anything and then pressing the post button. This way they can ask for help and suggestions or guidance from other users.

Connecting with the user

Users can search for people in the search bar and apply some filters to minimize the search results and when they want to connect with that user then they can directly connect with them by pressing the connect icon on the profile card or can open the profile and then connect by pressing the button at the bottom.

Categorizing the users

When the user wants to connect with someone by pressing the connect icon then they will be redirected to the category page under connection where they can categorize the users to any of the default 6 categories and a pop-up will show to confirm the action. They can add the users to a particular category by opening that category and pressing the ‘Add new user’ beside the search bar.

Refering the users to others

Users can refer their connection to others if they are satisfied with their help and someone else needs their help by pressing the refer icon under the profile of the user. Users can refer other user profiles to the people who need help as a response to their status posted by clicking on the refer button below the status.
